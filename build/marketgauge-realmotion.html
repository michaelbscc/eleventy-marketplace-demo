 How It Works

To install any of our free ACP plug-ins, simply log in to your account, click the install button up above and the plug-in will be instantly added to your ACP experience. The next time you visit ACP, you'll find the contents of your new plug-in right there waiting for you.

For any of our for-purchase plug-ins, the process works in a very similar fashion. When you click the "Buy Now" button, a checkout window will pop up on top of the page. Follow the payment steps there to purchase the plug-in, and it will be automatically installed into your account as soon as your checkout is complete.
